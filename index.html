About Us

WHO WE ARE?

TaRaTiTaY is a nickname that they call to me, Where I feel Unique.My product is all made by myself,
I put my heart and mind into it. To fully see my talent and to share it with others,
The joy that brings me every time I create and see things that, I feel like I'am the only one have it.
So thats why they ask, Why HandMade are very expensive? The answer is we workhard on it even in the small details.

TaRaTiTaY offers a great example of harmonious living where beads, ribbon and resin of various colors,
originating from different geographic locations, come together to create a beautiful piece.
TaRaTiTaY is an effort to spread the thread of love and harmony by taking a cue from beaded necklaces and earrings.

We believe that each individual has a unique expression of beauty and they should be able to express that in the jewelry
they wear or any think they have.
To help facilitate your expression, TaRaTiTaY allows you to design your own item as per your desired specifications.
When you shop at TaRaTiTaY, you get to create your own beaded&resin jewelry also personalize flower bouquet
that are unique to your style and personality.



Be part of our satisfide costumers and have your own design item.
